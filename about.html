About Mulanga Tutoring

Our Story

Mulanga Tutoring was founded in 2025 by a passionate educator who recognized the need for personalized, high-quality tutoring in Mathematics and Science.

We began serving learners in Limpopo and later expanded to online tutoring, reaching students across South Africa.

Experience

Over the past year, our tutor has gained extensive experience in high school curricula, helping learners improve their confidence and academic performance.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible tutoring tailored to each student’s learning style through one-on-one and online sessions.

We value patience, empathy, dedication, and excellence.
